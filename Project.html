About Us

Welcome to our Internet of Things (IoT) project aimed at supporting modern agriculture. Our goal is to provide real-time data monitoring of essential environmental factors like humidity, temperature, and soil moisture to help farmers make informed decisions.

With our innovative sensor technology, we strive to enhance crop yield, optimize resource use, and promote sustainable farming practices.
